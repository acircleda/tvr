About

Have you ever wanted to watch your favorite show but cannot decide which episode to watch? I built pickmy.show to solve this very problem! This is a personal project of a computer nerd and TV junkie who couldn't decide which Futurama episode to watching - having seen each one a hundred times.

By the way, any ad revenue or donations go directly toward maintence and hosting costs.

If you have made it this far, thanks for stopping by! I hope you have enjoyed the app.
